Our Products

We Offer Reliable Industrial Bearing Products

We are authorized distributors of NSK India Ltd. and NIBC bearings, offering a wide range of high-quality industrial bearing solutions for diverse applications.

Bearings

Engineered in ball and roller styles, these versatile components efficiently manage both high-speed rotations and heavy-duty loads for any application.

Super Precision Bearings

Super precision bearings utilize specialized materials and manufacturing accuracy to deliver superior speed, precision, and service life.

Bearing Units

Ball bearing units combine a bearing, housing, and sealing components into a standardized form used in key parts of equipment.

Maintenance Products

Grease, bearing heaters, and more for supporting proper maintenance of industrial bearings and machinery.

Bearing Accessories

Essential components like housings, sleeves, and seals designed to simplify installation, protect your bearings, and extend their lifespan.

Special Environment Bearings (SPACEA™)

Offers exceptional performance tailored to demanding environments like vacuum, and corrosive conditions.

Ball Screws

Ball Screw is produced from the latest tribology (friction control technology) for precision linear motion.

NSK Linear Guides™

NSK Linear Guide manufactured by production technology pursuing high precision and high quality for machine tools.
